This is a group for folks interested in QGIS, the free, open-source Geographic Information System software. We are located in the Puget Sound Region of Washington State.

All skill levels are welcome! We will periodically meet to share topics of interest. Bring your questions, or share examples with the rest of the group. The idea is to learn more about this very capable mapping and analysis package.

Check out our blog for QGIS tips and information about our meetings.

You can join us by signing up for the meetup group at meetup.org.
